Welcome to Full Court Stats

Full Court Stats is your all-access pass to the world of custom basketball analytics. It's the ultimate platform for NBA fans, data enthusiasts, and aspiring analysts to create and share personalized NBA statistics that reflect what you value most in the game.

  • Create and share your own custom NBA stats, based on what matters most to you: scoring, efficiency, awards, or clutch performance.
  • Explore interactive and creative data visualizations that bring player and team trends to life.
  • Download NBA stats data for your own deep dives or projects.
  • View season predictions powered by analytics, machine learning, and community insight.

Whether you're building your perfect MVP metric or looking for a fresh perspective on the league, Full Court Stats gives you the tools and freedom to see basketball your way.
